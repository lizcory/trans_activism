Anti-Transgender Legislation on the Rise

Only three months into 2021, US state legislators have introduced an historic number of new bills that threaten the rights of transgender people — 93, as of this writing. During the 2020 session, a staggering 79 anti-transgender bills were filed, the previous record-high.

Several of the bills in the current session specifically target trans children, who would be barred from accessing gender-affirming healthcare and participating in athletics with their cisgender peers.

This wave of discriminatory policy has ignited public discourse and calls for action have resounded on Twitter under the hashtag #TransRightsAreHumanRights.

Legislation Tracker

Anti-transgender legislation is not just increasing but changing in type. While in 2018 and 2019 bills legislating bathrooms were more common, legislation in 2020 and 2021 largely focuses on participation in sports and healthcare access for transgender children.
