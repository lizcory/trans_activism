Methodology: Tweets were gathered from a full archive search of the Twitter API v2. We queried tweets from 1/02/20 - 3/26/21 that included "#TransRightsAreHumanRights" and filtered for those containing location information at the city level. Coordinates for each tweet were calculated by first averaging the bounding box coordinates marking the city and then applying a random jitter to avoid point overlap. Anti-trans bill information was gathered from the ACLU's page on Legislation Affecting LGBTQ Rights Across the Country.
